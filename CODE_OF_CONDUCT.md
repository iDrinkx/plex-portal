# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

To help keep this project useful and maintainable, contributors are expected to:

- be respectful and constructive
- assume good intent
- give technical feedback clearly and factually
- avoid harassment, insults, discrimination, and personal attacks
- keep discussions focused on the project

## Unacceptable behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable in issues, pull requests, discussions, or other project spaces:

- abusive or hostile language
- harassment or intimidation
- discrimination of any kind
- sharing private information without permission
- spam, trolling, or deliberately disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, remove, or reject contributions and comments that do not follow this code of conduct.

Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent restrictions from participating in the project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer privately.

For security-related reports, use the process described in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).
